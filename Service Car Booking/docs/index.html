Plan and Record Service Car Bookings

Prepare agenda, invite users and record minutes of a Service Car Booking

Install

From your site

To install this app, login to your site and click on "Installer". Search for Service Car Booking and click on "Install"

Using Bench

Go to your bench folder and setup the new app

$ bench get-app Service Car Booking https://github.com/frappe/Service Car Booking
$ bench new-site testsite
$ bench --site testsite install-app Service Car Booking

Login to your site to configure the app.
